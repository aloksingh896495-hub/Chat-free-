Study Tips

🧠
Level Up Your Studying

Proven techniques used by top students to score higher with less stress.

⏱️

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, break for 5. After 4 cycles, take a 20-minute break. Use our built-in timer!

🔁

Active Recall

Instead of rereading notes, close them and try to recall key points from memory. This forces your brain to retrieve information, strengthening memory.

📅

Spaced Repetition

Review topics at increasing intervals (1 day → 3 days → 1 week → 2 weeks). This fights the forgetting curve and cements knowledge long-term.

✍️

The Feynman Technique

Explain a concept in simple language as if teaching a child. Where you get stuck is exactly where your understanding has gaps — fill those first.

😴

Sleep is Non-Negotiable

Your brain consolidates memories during sleep. Studying late into the night and sacrificing sleep is counterproductive. Aim for 7–8 hours every night.

🎯

Eliminate Distractions

Put your phone in another room, use website blockers, and let your family know your study hours. A distraction-free environment doubles your efficiency.
